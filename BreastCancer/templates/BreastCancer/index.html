Information about the Breast Cancer

Worldwide Public Health

Breast cancer is the most common type of cancer among women which is highly heterogeneous, and caused by the interplay of hereditary and environmental risk factors . It is caused by irregular growth of cells in the breast tissue, which leads to the creation of tumors that could be benign (non-cancerous) or malignant (cancerous). Based on the statistics published by the World Health Organization, one in 8 to 10 women suffers from breast cancer. Breast cancer can be categorized into two, which are malignant breast cancer and benign breast cancer.
